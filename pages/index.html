Projects

Embedding Space Viewer
Interactive Voronoi map of 485 word embeddings projected onto custom semantic axes. Explores how distributional semantics organizes words in high-dimensional space.
Dot Product Visualizer
Interactive 2D canvas for exploring the dot product. Drag two vectors to see projection, angle, and the geometry behind A·B in real time.
Cross Product Visualizer
Interactive 2D canvas for the cross product. Drag two vectors to see the parallelogram area, rotation direction, and how A×B relates to 3D orientation.

About

I study the geometry of embedding spaces — how language models organize meaning, where they succeed, and where they systematically fail. My current work maps the internal structure of embedding models to understand why vector-similarity retrieval breaks down on logical reasoning tasks.

I also work on Wikidata integration for Japanese cultural heritage, building structured linked-data pipelines for Shinto shrines and historical records.
